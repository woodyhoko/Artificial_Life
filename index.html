Artificial Life · Neuroevolution

Life, as it could be.

A 2D ecosystem of neural-network creatures with raycast senses and an energy budget. With no hand-coded rules, evolution alone discovers grazing, pursuit, evasion and territorial behaviour — emergent predator/prey dynamics straight out of Lotka-Volterra.

Emergent strategies
Not programmed — attractors of the evolutionary dynamics.
🌿

Grazing

The network learns to steer toward grass-proximity raycast signals.

🦅

Pursuit

Asymmetric energy means stronger creatures learn to approach weaker ones.

💨

Evasion

Lighter creatures learn to turn away from the proximity signal of a stronger neighbour.

📍

Territory

Creatures near high-regrowth patches learn to loiter and hold ground.
